ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in Bangkok Thailand, and moved to USA at the age of 18. I am a US Citizen and am bilingual, able to read and write in both English and Thai. I continued to college right after highschool and became interested in Computer Science. I graduated college with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science, and have been interested in Front End Development since. I am a Space enthusiast and love listening to music. Discovering new songs and bands feels almost as good as when I am coding.
